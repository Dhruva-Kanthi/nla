About Us

**About Us** We’re a group of high school students who saw two problems at once. Local small businesses needed extra hands and fresh ideas, and our classmates were hunting for real-world experience that most internships don’t offer until college. So we built a nonprofit that tackles both. Our team pairs student volunteers with neighborhood shops, cafés, start-ups, and family-run services. Students get on-the-ground projects in marketing, finance, tech, or whatever the business needs. Owners get motivated help they wouldn’t otherwise afford. Everyone wins: businesses grow, students learn, and our community becomes a little stronger. If you’re a student ready to trade textbook theory for hands-on work, or a business owner who could use extra brainpower, let’s talk. We’re here to learn, help, and prove that high schoolers can drive real change.
