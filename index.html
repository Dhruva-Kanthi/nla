About Us

We’re a group of high school students who saw two problems at once. Local small businesses needed extra hands and fresh ideas, and our classmates were hunting for real-world experience that is very hard to find in high school. So we built a nonprofit that tackles both.

More info on about page.

Contact Us

Questions, ideas, or just curious about what we do? Email us at nextlineadvisory@gmail.com. Tell us a little about yourself—whether you’re a small-business owner looking for extra help or a student searching for hands-on experience—and we’ll point you in the right direction.

We’re based in Frisco, Texas, but you don’t have to be. If you’re outside our hometown and want to get involved, reach out anyway; many of our projects can be handled remotely or adapted to other communities.

We answer every message, usually within two school days. If you’d like a call, add your phone number to the email and let us know the best time to reach you. We look forward to hearing from you.
